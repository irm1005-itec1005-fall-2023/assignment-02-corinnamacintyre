    About the Members

    Will "Wilbur Soot" Gold

    Will Gold, also known as Wilbur Soot on Twitch and Youtube, is the lead singer and creator of the band. Before he formed the current top charting indie rock band Lovejoy, he had a small run as a solo artist with his two EPs, one called Maybe I Was Boring (2019) and the other called Your City Gave Me Asthma (2020).

    Ash Kabosu

    Ash rocks the bass and weilds the vlog camera. Many of the bands adventures are documented through vlogs which the silent Kabosu doesn't even talk in! Funnily enough, Wilbur had seen him randomly in a coffee shop with a guitar strapped to his back and asked if he wanted to join a band. The rest is history.

    Mark Boardman

    Mark is the drummer of the band. They actually hired him from Fivvr for what was supposed to be a one time gig, but he meshed so well with the group that he became a permanent member!

    Joe Goldsmith

    Joe plays the lead guitar and as one of Will's oldest friends writes the lyrics and sings backup. He was one of the first people Wilbur turned to when he started up Lovejoy, I guess you could say the Gold's stick together!

    Rise to Fame

    Lovejoys rise to fame was a unique one. Majority of the traction they had was from Wilbur Soot's Twitch and Youtube audience, however after the release of a few EPs they ended up finding an even bigger pool of people that were interested!
